# 🛍️ Customer Purchasing Behavior Analysis
# Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior in a retail dataset to:

* Increase revenue
* Improve customer retention
* Optimize marketing strategies

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains customer-level transaction data, including:

* Demographics (age, gender, location)
* Purchase details (items, category, amount)
* Behavioral attributes (purchase frequency, previous purchases)
* Marketing factors (discounts, subscription status, shipping type)

## 🧹 Data Preprocessing

* Removed duplicates
* Renamed columns for consistency and readability
* Checked for missing values
* Structured data for analysis

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Revenue distribution across categories
* Customer demographics analysis
* Purchase frequency patterns
* Top-performing products and categories

---

## 🧠 Customer Segmentation (FM Analysis)

* **Monetary (M):** Total spend per customer
* **Frequency (F):** Number of transactions per customer

Customers were segmented into:

* Top Customers
* High-Value Customers
* Mid-Value Customers
* Low-Value Customers

##  Key Insights

* Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category
* High-value customers contribute the majority of total revenue
* Frequent buyers have significantly higher spending behavior
* Discounts positively influence purchase amounts
* Subscription users tend to spend more

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

### 🚀 Revenue Growth

* Focus on high-value customer segments
* Introduce product bundling strategies in top categories

### 🔁 Customer Retention

* Target low-frequency customers with personalized offers
* Implement loyalty programs for repeat customers

### 🎯 Marketing Optimization

* Use customer segmentation for targeted campaigns
* Personalize recommendations based on purchase behavior
